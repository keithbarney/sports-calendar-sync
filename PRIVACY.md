# Privacy Policy — Sports Calendar Sync

**Effective date:** 2026-04-22

Sports Calendar Sync is built to respect your privacy.

## What we collect

Nothing. The app has no servers, no analytics, no accounts, and no tracking.

## What stays on your device

- Followed teams (SwiftData, local only)
- App preferences (UserDefaults)
- Calendar events you choose to create (stored by iOS in your Apple Calendar)

## Third-party services

- **ESPN public API** — used to fetch team lists, schedules, and scores. No personal data is sent. See ESPN's privacy policy for how they handle public API traffic.
- **Apple EventKit** — used to read and write your calendar. Data stays on your device / iCloud.

Changes to this policy will be posted here with an updated effective date.
